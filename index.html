About the Summit

Dear Esteemed Leaders, We are honored to invite you to the Asia Pacific Lions Leaders Summit 2026, a prestigious gathering of visionary leaders dedicated to service, impact, Leadership and global unity. Set in the heart of Sibu, Sarawak Borneo, from 17th to 19th July 2026, this summit is your gateway to inspiration, collaboration, Leadership and cultural celebration on an international stage.

Our Theme: "Uniting Leaders, Inspiring Service"
